About Travel Mithra

Welcome to Travel Mithra, your dedicated platform for accessing reliable and up-to-date bus and train timetables in the vibrant city of Dakshina Kannada. We understand the importance of efficient public transport for daily commutes and city exploration. Our goal is to provide you with a seamless experience to find the information you need quickly and easily.

Our platform aggregates data on bus routes operated by the Karnataka State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) and train schedules relevant to the South Karnataka region. We strive to ensure the accuracy of the information presented, but please note that timetables and routes can be subject to change. Always refer to official transport authority sources for the most up-to-date details.

We are continuously working to improve our services and data coverage. Future enhancements may include features such as live updates, route planning tools, and integration with other transport modes. Your feedback is valuable to us as we strive to make navigating Bengaluru's public transport system as smooth as possible.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or encounter any issues, please feel free to contact us. Thank you for using Travel Mithra!
